# Application Concept – StockSync

## Application
StockSync is a multi-channel inventory intelligence and analytics platform that serves as a centralized "Source of Truth" for modern retailers. It unifies stock data from Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop, and physical POS systems into a single dashboard, utilizing real-time synchronization and geographic heatmapping to eliminate inventory fragmentation and maximize profitability.

## Core Features
- **Real-Time Multi-Channel Sync:** Sub-15-second inventory updates across all APIs with automated safety stock locking.
- **Geographic Sales Heatmapping:** Interactive Mapbox integration to visualize sales density and identify high-opportunity "Hot Zones."
- **Centralized Intelligence Dashboard:** Unified view of revenue, profit margins, and ROI per channel, including "Dead Stock" identification.
- **Predictive Stock Forecasting:** AI-driven "Days of Cover" alerts and automated Purchase Order (PO) drafts based on historical sales velocity.

## Users
- **E-commerce Founders:** Seeking a bird's-eye view of total business health and multi-platform revenue.
- **Inventory & Operations Managers:** Responsible for stock accuracy, warehouse coordination, and preventing overselling.
- **Growth & Marketing Specialists:** Analyzing geographic trends to optimize localized ad spend and promotions.
- **Finance & C-Suite:** Monitoring high-level profitability, platform fees, and market expansion opportunities.

## Constraints
- **Scalability:** Support for up to 50,000 SKUs and 100,000 monthly orders per client with < 2.5s load times.
- **API Management:** Strict compliance with third-party rate limits (e.g., Amazon’s throttling rules) and webhook-based event capturing.
- **Security & Compliance:** Full GDPR/CCPA compliance regarding customer location data and encrypted API credential storage.
- **Platform Integrations:** Mandatory connectivity with Shopify, Amazon SP-API, TikTok Shop, and physical POS systems (Square/Clover).
