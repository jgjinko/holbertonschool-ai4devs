# UI Concept - StockSync

## Purpose
Unify fragmented inventory data from multiple sales channels (Shopify, Amazon, TikTok Shop, and Physical POS) into a single, interactive intelligence hub. The platform eliminates "ghost inventory," prevents stockouts via AI-driven forecasting, and optimizes marketing spend through geographic sales heatmapping.

## Users
- **E-commerce Founders:** Maintain a bird’s-eye view of profitability and multi-platform revenue performance.
- **Inventory Managers:** Ensure stock accuracy across multiple warehouses and automate reorder points to avoid stockouts.
- **Growth Specialists:** Identify geographic "hot zones" to optimize localized advertising spend and fulfillment expansion.
- **Finance & C-Suite:** Reconcile platform fees, shipping costs, and COGS against real-time sales for true ROI visibility.

## Core Screens
- **Command Center (Dashboard):** A modular home screen featuring high-level KPIs (Total Revenue, Active Alerts, Sync Latency) and AI-powered stock velocity widgets.
- **Inventory Manager ("My Stocks"):** A high-density data table for managing SKUs across all channels, featuring "Quick Look" side panels for granular stock history.
- **Geographic Intelligence (Map):** A full-bleed Mapbox integration visualizing sales density, shipping costs, and proximity to fulfillment centers.
- **Integration Hub:** A technical management area to connect, monitor, and troubleshoot API links for Shopify, Amazon SP-API, and TikTok Shop.

## Actions
- **Real-Time Monitoring:** View live sync status (<15s latency) and receive instant "Safety Stock" breach alerts.
- **Predictive Reordering:** Generate automated Purchase Order (PO) drafts based on AI-calculated "Days of Cover" metrics.
- **Spatial Analysis:** Filter heatmaps by SKU, date range, or channel to identify high-opportunity geographic zones.
- **Conflict Resolution:** Manually override or reconcile inventory discrepancies between physical POS systems and digital storefronts.
