# UI Concept - StockSync

## Purpose
Unify fragmented inventory data from multiple sales channels into a single "Source of Truth" to eliminate ghost inventory and optimize supply chains through predictive analytics.

## Users
- **E-commerce Founders:** Monitor multi-platform revenue performance and bird's-eye brand profitability.
- **Inventory Managers:** Maintain stock accuracy across diverse warehouses and automate reorder points to prevent overselling.

## Core Screens
- **Command Center:** Modular dashboard with high-level KPIs, sync status indicators, and AI stock velocity alerts.
- **Inventory Manager:** High-density data table for managing SKUs across all connected platforms with "Quick Look" side panels.
- **Geographic Intelligence:** Interactive Mapbox-powered heatmaps visualizing sales density and localized marketing opportunities.

## Actions
- **Real-Time Syncing:** Monitor multi-channel inventory updates with a target latency of <15 seconds.
- **Predictive Restocking:** View AI-generated "Days of Cover" alerts and generate automated purchase order drafts.
- **Geographic Filtering:** Analyze sales zones by SKU, date range, or channel to optimize advertising spend.
