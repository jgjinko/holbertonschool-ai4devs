# Product Idea: CarpoolConnect


## Vision

Enable companies to significantly reduce their carbon footprint and employee transportation costs through intelligent carpooling matching, while fostering workplace community and supporting sustainability goals.


## Users

- **Corporate Employees**: Want to find colleagues with similar commute routes to share rides, reduce fuel costs, and build workplace relationships.
- **Sustainability Officers**: Need to track and report on carbon footprint reduction from carpooling to measure progress toward corporate environmental goals.
- **Facility Managers**: Want to optimize parking space usage and reduce the costs associated with parking infrastructure and congestion.
- **HR Managers**: Need to demonstrate tangible employee benefits and cost savings to improve workplace satisfaction and retention.


## Key Features

- **Smart Commute Matching**: Algorithm-driven pairing based on 2-mile radius, $\pm$ 30-minute schedule flexibility, and vehicle capacity.
- **Enterprise SSO & Integration**: Seamless access via corporate identity providers (Okta, Azure AD) for a secure, "walled-garden" employee environment.
- **Environmental Impact Tracking**: Real-time calculation of $CO_2$ savings per trip with automated reporting for corporate ESG metrics.
- **Parking Optimization**: Dynamic allocation of reserved, preferred parking spots for active carpoolers integrated with facility gate systems.
- **Cost Savings Analytics**: Individual and aggregate dashboards comparing carpool costs against public transit and single-occupancy vehicle expenses.
- **Enterprise-Grade Security (Differentiator)**: Unlike public apps, it features SOC 2 Type II compliance and direct integration with corporate HRIS for automated user verification.
