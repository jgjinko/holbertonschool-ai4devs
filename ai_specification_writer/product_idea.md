# Product Idea: CarpoolConnect


## Vision

To transform the daily corporate commute into a sustainable, cost-effective, and community-building experience by leveraging intelligent matching and deep enterprise integration.


## Users

- **Corporate Employees**: Seek to reduce commuting costs and build workplace relationships through shared rides with verified colleagues.
- **Sustainability Officers**: Require verifiable data on CO2 reduction to track and report on progress toward corporate environmental goals.


## Key Features

- **Smart Commute Matching**: An AI-driven algorithm that pairs colleagues based on route proximity, schedule flexibility, and vehicle capacity.
- **Enterprise SSO Integration**: Seamless, secure access via corporate identity providers (Okta, Azure AD) to ensure a verified, "walled-garden" environment.
- **Environmental Impact Tracking**: Real-time calculation of CO2 savings per trip with automated reporting for corporate sustainability dashboards.
